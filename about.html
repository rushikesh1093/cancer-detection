Preventative Measures Against Cancer

Reduce your risk of cancer by adopting healthy lifestyle habits. Eat a balanced diet rich in fruits and vegetables, maintain a healthy weight, exercise regularly, avoid tobacco and excessive alcohol consumption, and protect yourself from the sun. Regular screenings and self-examinations can also help detect cancer early when treatment is most effective.
